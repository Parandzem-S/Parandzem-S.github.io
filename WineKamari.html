Kamari Wine Story

The Kamari wine production was started in 2017. The winery is localted in village Rind, Vayots dzor marz which even Bloomberg recognized as one of the ancient places of the wine production.

Characteristics of Areni grape

The Areni grape is an amber black coloured grape having a thick skin.
This grape is extremely disease resistant & is well adapted to the strong diurnal variations in temperature.This is a key quality for a grape cultivated in harsh continental climates. (For more information click here)

Areni grape is used in Kamari Wine production for the following reasons:

  1. This grape gives a pleasant sourish and a specific taste and hence it makes very good wines.
  2. Areni grape is of aborigine sort which has over a period of time adapted to the climate of Vayots Dzor, where our facory is located.The grape does not grow so good in any other place in the whole of Armenia.
  3. The tough and robust skin of this grape not only maintains a structural integrity, but also helps retain a degree of freshness and elegance which is a must in a wine grape.
